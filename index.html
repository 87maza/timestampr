The Unix Time Converter

Welcome to the Unix time conversion app!

This app will convert Unix time to human dates and vice-versa

Please enter either the unix time or human calendar dates into the url and our scientists will convert the rest for ya. Service is open 24/7

Conversions:

  • At the end of the URL, please enter a human date ie. (December 12, 2012 / 12-12-12 / Dec 12, 2012)
  • You can also convert the epoch date to human dates ie. (1355270400)

ps. avoid using slashes in the url "/" or "\". They don't mix well with the formulas =(
